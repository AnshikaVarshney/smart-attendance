Welcome to SMART Attendance

The attendance app is an app by Mutiara Smart Computing Sdn Bhd for recording the attendance of its staffs whenever and whereever. It is an extension for the Smart Leave and Attendance Management System (SMARTLA).

Functions

SMART Attendance is able to record the attendance of a staff. The recorded attendance will be based on the time of their arrival and also their location. Hence, the company will be able to monitor the whereabout of their staffs. The attendance record will be also made available in the SMARTLA.

Account

SMART Attendance required the user to be a staff of Mutiara Smart Computing Sdn Bhd since the user need to input their Staff ID and Password to log into the app and use the attendance function.
