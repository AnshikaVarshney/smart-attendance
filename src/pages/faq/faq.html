What to do if when I click on the "Punch In" or "Punch Out" button, my location cannot be detected? Answer:
This happens due to the hardware problem in the device. There are several workaround for it.

Method 1 - Switch off your GPS, let the app switch it on when detecting your location.
Method 2 - Reboot your phone.
Method 3 - Uninstall the app, then reinstall the app.
If the problem persists, please contact administrator by using the Support Form.
